You have the right to live free from violence, regardless of gender. Domestic violence is a crime, and laws exist to protect survivors. Rights include:

  • Protection orders (restraining orders)
  • Right to free legal aid
  • Right to report abuse without discrimination
  • Right to shelter and safety
